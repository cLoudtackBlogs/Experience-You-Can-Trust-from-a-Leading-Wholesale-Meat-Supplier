Why Businesses Choose Pitco Foods as Their Wholesale Meat Supplier

When sourcing meat in large volumes, reliability and quality aren’t just preferences—they’re business necessities. Whether you’re running a deli counter, preparing daily specials, or stocking your butcher case, the meat you order needs to arrive on time, in excellent condition, and prepared exactly how you need it.
We work hard to deliver on that expectation by focusing on the details that matter:

Product Quality

We source our meat products from trusted suppliers who follow industry best practices for safety, animal handling, and processing. Every cut we carry is selected for consistency, appearance, and flavor. We stock USDA-inspected beef, pork, poultry, lamb, and more, offering a range of grades and specifications to meet the needs of various customers.
From restaurant-grade tenderloins to economical bulk items for grocers, our team understands what to look for and what our customers expect.

Consistent Availability

We maintain a broad inventory and streamlined ordering process so our customers can count on steady supply. In the meat business, missed deliveries or unexpected substitutions can quickly become costly. That’s why we keep our warehouses well-stocked and our communication clear. Our goal is to help your operations run smoothly—not keep you guessing.

Tailored Service

Every customer has a different set of needs, and we don’t believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. Need custom cuts or special packaging? Looking for seasonal items or regionally preferred products? We work with you to understand what matters most to your business and find solutions that make sense.

Our Product Line: Wide Variety with Reliable Sourcing

Our selection reflects the diversity of the customers we serve. Whether you’re a chef preparing high-end dishes or a grocer stocking staples for family dinners, we provide the cuts and formats that fit your business.

Beef

We offer a full range of beef products including USDA Prime, Choice, and Select grades. Our inventory includes whole muscles, ground beef, short ribs, stew meat, and specialty roasts.

Pork

From shoulder and loin cuts to belly, ribs, and sausage ingredients, our pork offerings are available fresh and frozen, packaged in bulk or in ready-to-sell portions.

Poultry

Our poultry selection includes fresh and frozen chicken, turkey, duck, and game birds. We provide options for boneless, skinless, whole, and specialty cuts based on your specific use.

Lamb and Goat

We support retailers and restaurants with demand for whole lamb, goat, and specialty cuts often used in Mediterranean, Middle Eastern, African, and South Asian cuisines.

Processed and Cured Meats

Our inventory includes bacon, hot dogs, sausages, smoked meats, and deli cuts—sourced from brands you trust, as well as private-label selections that deliver strong value.

Maintaining High Standards in Food Safety

Nothing matters more than food safety. That’s why we’ve invested in the people, equipment, and protocols that ensure your meat arrives fresh, clean, and properly handled.

Our facilities are inspected regularly and meet state and federal requirements for temperature control, storage, and handling. We train our staff to follow careful food safety procedures and partner only with vendors who do the same.

Each product we carry is traceable through the supply chain, with labeling and documentation that meet all compliance standards. That traceability adds an extra layer of security and transparency for our customers—and their customers in turn.

Serving Foodservice Operations

Restaurant owners, chefs, and catering managers know how fast-paced the kitchen can be. Our job is to make their jobs easier. We do that by offering:

  • Reliable deliveries on a schedule that fits your prep cycle
  • Consistent sizing and trimming that reduces back-of-house labor
  • Advice on cost-per-serving and product selection
  • Support for sudden changes in menu or volume

Supporting Independent Retailers and Grocers

Our retail customers count on us for freshness, packaging, and product display consistency. A meat case is often one of the first things shoppers see when they enter a store—it needs to look appealing and meet expectations for quality and value. We provide:

  • Eye-catching product packaging suitable for display
  • Standard and custom cuts for cultural or seasonal demand
  • Competitive pricing that supports profit margins
  • Clear labeling for ease of stocking and selling

We also work closely with stores serving specific communities, providing culturally preferred products such as goat, oxtail, and halal-certified meats.

Responsive Customer Support

What sets us apart isn’t just what we carry—it’s how we serve. Our team is responsive, informed, and always ready to help. Whether you’re placing an order, tracking a shipment, or asking about new product options, we treat your questions with urgency and respect.
